## Summary

Broomva is not a SaaS. The components are named in Greek and Latin on purpose (`arcan`, `lago`, `anima`, `haima`, `prosopon`, `vigil`, `autonomic`); the system is modeled biologically; the central thesis is a stability proof for recursive controlled systems with documented λ values per level. The commercial visual identity has to honor that — not market against it.

This brief commits to a single iconic asset family — **Plate I. *Anima Toroidalis*** — a scholarly anatomical-plate composition presenting broomva's central mathematical object: a toroidal attractor with two interleaved internal flows and a cross-folded throat constriction, annotated in Greek, dressed with the actual RCS stability values from `CLAUDE.md`, set on warm cream paper with warm-black ink and a single sanguine-red spot color.

Three deliverables in one visual language:

- **Hero (16:9)** for broomva.tech and OG / link previews
- **Social (1:1)** for paid placements (X, LinkedIn)
- **Video (10s, 16:9, native audio)** of the attractor *converging* — the proof animating itself

Execution is contemporary-clean: pure vector rendering, no fake paper aging, no scanned-document noise. The classical sensibility lives in *composition* and *hierarchy*, not in distressed textures.

## Goals

- Broomva has a **canonical icon** — the toroidal anima attractor — that scales from a 1:1 social ad to a 32-foot trade-show wall and remains recognizable.
- Every visible number traces back to a verifiable line in the broomva workspace (λ values from `~/broomva/CLAUDE.md` RCS table; theorem reference to `research/rcs/papers/p0-foundations/main.tex`).
- The visual identity reads as *scholarship*, not as *marketing*. Tagline "Building systems that last" appears once, in publisher's-imprint register, never as a floating overlay.
- The 10s video closes on the same composition as the still hero — making the video the still's *earned* form.
- No other agent-infrastructure brand looks like this in 2026 — the bet on δ (contemporary execution, classical sensibility) is intentional and not hedged.

## Non-goals

- Not a brand-system redesign. Covers commercial visual assets only — does not propose new logos, palettes for the broomva.tech site, code-doc typography, or any product UI changes.
- No human figures in any frame.
- Not a multi-treatment campaign. The previous 4-treatment exploration is explicitly replaced.
- Not a long-form video. The 10s ceiling is fixed by Kling 2.6's max duration and by the "single beat" commercial-discipline principle.

## The World — Cybernetic Tradition × δ Register

**Direction (A+B):** the cybernetic tradition — the post-WWII flowering when Wiener, Ashby, Beer, von Foerster, McCulloch, Pitts, Maturana, and Varela treated organisms, machines, and societies as *the same kind of thing*: controlled systems with feedback. D'Arcy Thompson before them, doing the same for morphology. Haeckel painting biological forms as mathematical taxonomies. Tufte and Anthropic interp inheriting the visual grammar.

**Register (δ):** contemporary execution, classical sensibility. Modern crispness, old-soul compositions. Like Anthropic hiring someone trained in 18th-century scientific illustration to do the figures.

**Center of gravity (ii + sparks of i):** math-first — phase-space, attractor, manifold, λ values are the subject — with sparks of body in the *register*: anatomical-plate composition, trajectories rendered with the care of vasculature, organ-shaped silhouette. D'Arcy Thompson's *On Growth and Form* (1917) is the patron saint of this exact blend.

## Section 1 — The Manifold

**Object:** the *Toroidal Anima* — a torus with two interleaved internal flows.

- **Major-axis** flow (slow, governing, the long arc) — labeled αἷμα / *haima*
- **Minor-axis** flow (fast, circulating, local homeostasis) — labeled αὐτόνομος / *autonomos*

**Throat:** a single **cross-folded constriction** at one point on the surface. A Möbius-like half-twist where the major axis folds back on itself — the topological signature of broomva's Level-3 self-referential closure (per `research/rcs/docs/self-referential-closure.md`).

**Trajectories:** rendered as vasculature — precise hairlines tracing major-axis flow, finer secondary lines tracing minor-axis circulation. The "sparks of i" live here: drawn with anatomical care, lines that feel like veins under glass.

**Markers:** four canonical points along the major-axis cycle pinned with λ stability values from the workspace `CLAUDE.md`:

- **L0** (External plant — `arcan` agent loop): λ₀ = 1.455
- **L1** (Agent internal — `autonomic`): λ₁ = 0.411
- **L2** (Meta-control — EGRI): λ₂ = 0.069
- **L3** (Governance — `CLAUDE.md` + `AGENTS.md` + policy): λ₃ = 0.006

These are the actual values, not decorative. Verifiable.

## Section 2 — Labeling System

Six typographic registers, in order of page weight:

### 1. Plate header *(top, centered, small caps)*

> **PLATE I.** *Anima Toroidalis*

Specimen-catalog convention. Implies Plate II, III, IV in a future line (haima cross-section, autonomic feedback loop, etc.).

### 2. Sub-header *(top, smaller, italic)*

> *Phase portrait of a recursive controlled system at stability.*
> *λ₀ = 1.455, λ₁ = 0.411, λ₂ = 0.069, λ₃ = 0.006. After Broomva (2026).*

Figure caption an educated reader expects. Terse, factual, no marketing.

### 3. Region labels *on the manifold* (Greek + Latin transliteration)

- **ψυχή** (psychē / *anima*) — at the central attractor basin
- **αἷμα** (haima) — along the major-axis flow
- **αὐτόνομος** (autonomos) — along the minor-axis loop
- **arcanum** — at the throat constriction

Greek script grants authority; transliteration grants legibility. No English glosses.

### 4. Right gutter — stability table *(tabular oldstyle figures in body face, hairline rules)*

```
LEVEL    LAYER                    λ        STABILITY
L0       External plant           1.455    exponential
L1       Agent internal           0.411    exponential
L2       Meta-control (EGRI)      0.069    exponential
L3       Governance               0.006    narrow margin
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Composite: λᵢ > 0 ∀i ⟹ stable    (Theorem 1)
```

The L3 row is the only red gloss in the table — `narrow margin` and `0.006` set in sanguine red. The honesty hook: the brand admits where its own margins are thinnest.

### 5. Left gutter — theorem fragment *(typeset block)*

> **Theorem 1** *(Composite Stability).* *Let f : X → X be the dynamics of a recursive controlled system over levels L₀ … L₃ with per-level Lyapunov exponents λᵢ. If λᵢ > 0 for all i, then the composite system is exponentially stable.*
>
> ▢

Citation underneath: `(rcs/papers/p0-foundations, Theorem 1)`. The "▢" is the Halmos tombstone — the register-cue of completed proof.

### 6. Imprint footer *(bottom, centered, small caps)*

> **BUILDING SYSTEMS THAT LAST**
> *broomva.tech · MMXXVI*

Tagline as a publisher's imprint, not as marketing. Roman-numeral year.

## Section 3 — Paper & Ink Register

**Palette I — Sanguine on Cream:**

- **Paper:** warm cream `#FBF5E9`. Flat, no texture noise.
- **Primary ink:** warm black `#1A1614`. Never pure `#000000`.
- **Spot color:** sanguine red `#A82A1F`. Used ONLY at three places:
    1. The throat constriction on the manifold
    2. The plate header *Plate I*
    3. The L3 row of the stability table

### Line weight hierarchy (clean vector throughout)

- **Primary** 0.75 pt — manifold silhouette, bounding rule that frames the plate
- **Secondary** 0.5 pt — major-axis trajectories, label leaders, table hairline above composite line
- **Hairline** 0.25 pt — minor-axis trajectories, interior shading of the manifold
- **Hairline-fine** 0.15 pt — annotation leader lines, gutter rules, marginalia underscores

### Type families

- **Display + body:** Adobe Caslon Pro. Acceptable alternates: Garamond Premier Pro, Cardo.
- **Greek:** GFS Didot.
- **Math (theorem fragment, ▢ tombstone, Greek variables):** STIX Two.
- **Tabular figures (stability table):** Caslon tabular oldstyle figures. No monospace. The minute IBM Plex Mono enters, the plate stops being a plate.

### Texture discipline

**No paper grain, no aging, no scanned-document noise, no coffee stains.** Classical-ness lives in *composition and hierarchy*. Execution stays contemporary-clean. This is δ's central discipline — break it and the brand slides into vintage-cosplay.

## Section 4 — Motion Design (10s Video)

### Arc — *"Anima Toroidalis converges"*

The 10-second timeline animates the system finding its attractor. Trajectories scatter at frame 0, organize over the duration, settle into the canonical hero composition by frame 10. The mathematical content of the spot is the convergence proof itself.

| Time | What's on screen | What's happening |
|------|------------------|------------------|
| 0:00 | Cream paper; faded plate-furniture (header, gutters, footer) already faded in; manifold silhouette barely ghosted; scattered trajectory fragments | Pre-stability |
| 0:02 | Major-axis flow (αἷμα) begins drawing in along the torus | L0 stabilizing; λ₀ value appears in table |
| 0:04 | Major-axis complete; minor-axis flow (αὐτόνομος) begins drawing in | L1 stabilizing; λ₁ appears; sustained low-string note (cello G3) fades in under ambient |
| 0:06 | Both flows complete; interior shading of torus establishes faintly | L2 stabilizing; λ₂ appears |
| 0:08 | Throat constriction resolves in sanguine red; plate header *Plate I. Anima Toroidalis* fades in | L3 stabilizing; λ₃ appears (in red, in table); cello note resolves to sustained held tone |
| 0:09–0:10 | All motion ceases; composition holds steady | Final frame = pixel-match of hero still |

### Camera

Slow contemplative zoom-in with gentle parallax drift. ~15% focal-distance closure over 10s. Subtle horizontal drift of 2–3%. Last 1.5s decelerates to a stop. Single take, no cuts. Reference move: Ken Burns at quarter speed with Anthropic-product-film dignity. *Scholar's lean,* not Apple-zoom.

### Audio — *Scholar's study at night*

Soft ambient room tone. Distant clock tick at irregular cadence. Faint paper rustle. At 0:04, a single sustained low-string note (cello, ~G3 or A3) fades in under the ambient and holds through the end — *one* note, never resolving into a chord, never becoming melody. Final two seconds: the note quiets but holds. Ends in silence. Sound is *context*, not narrative.

### Frame logic

- **Opening frame** = hero still *depopulated.* Plate-furniture present but ghosted; manifold silhouette barely visible; trajectories not yet drawn.
- **Closing frame** = pixel-matched to hero still.
- **Last 1.5 seconds** hold steady (no continued motion) so the eye lands on the final composition.

## Execution Model

Two-pass pipeline. AI generates the substrate; type is composited in post.

### Pass 1 — AI substrate generation

- **Model:** Seedream 4.5 (`seedream_v4_5`) — Higgsfield's vector-illustration-capable model — first choice. **Nano Banana Pro** (`nano_banana_2`) is the escape hatch if Seedream 4.5 trips up.
- **Bypass the product-photoshoot enhancer.** Call `higgsfield generate create` directly. The enhancer homogenizes toward marketing-banner and trips the `gpt_image_2` NSFW filter on anything resembling biological line work — both failure modes of the previous run.
- **Substrate output:** torus on cream paper with trajectory linework, throat constriction marked, plate-furniture positioned but with placeholder text. Pure vector aesthetic, no fake aging.

### Pass 2 — Type compositing (manual)

- Set actual Greek labels in GFS Didot, body type in Caslon, math in STIX Two
- Position the stability table with real tabular oldstyle figures
- Set theorem fragment with real Halmos tombstone
- Set imprint footer in publisher-mark register
- Output: final hero still (16:9) and final social still (1:1, center-crop or native re-gen at 1:1)

### Pass 3 — Video generation

- **Model:** Kling 2.6 (`kling2_6`) — confirmed working on the current starter plan, supports `--image` from hero still, native audio synthesis
- **Input:** hero still (or the AI substrate before final type compositing — whichever lands cleaner) as `--image` start frame
- **Prompt** encodes arc, camera move, audio register
- **Output:** 10s MP4 with native audio
- **Post:** type labels animated in over the 10s arc per Section 4 timeline. Final 1.5s holds.

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Seedream 4.5 produces wormy/biological trajectory motion instead of geometric/mathematical | Prompt hard on "precise geometric lines, vector-clean, drawing themselves with discipline"; 2–3 generations expected to land a clean one |
| AI text rendering produces garbled Greek / λ values / theorem | Two-pass pipeline — text is composited in post, never trusted to the AI generator |
| Kling 2.6 audio synthesis interprets "cello note" as something else | Worst-case: generate silent, lay audio in post |
| Sanguine red over-applied during execution drift | Three-place discipline encoded in this brief (throat, plate header, L3 row); reviewer checks before final |
| Tagline overlay creep | Tagline appears ONCE, in imprint register, at the bottom edge. Never as floating overlay over the manifold. |
| Manifold shape unfaithful to actual RCS proof geometry | If the canonical attractor surface is documented in `research/rcs/papers/`, override and honor the proof directly. Otherwise the designed torus is the homage. |
| AI generator NSFW filter still flags the trajectory-as-vasculature render | Seedream 4.5 was chosen specifically to bypass this; fallback to Nano Banana Pro |
| Type compositing slips into different family by accident | Caslon + GFS Didot + STIX Two locked. Single source of typographic truth for the campaign. |
