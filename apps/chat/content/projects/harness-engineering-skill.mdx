## Problem

Teams over-focus on prompts and under-invest in the harness around the model.

## Approach

The skill packages reusable patterns for:

- architecture boundaries and contracts
- deterministic checks in CI
- observability defaults
- low-entropy runbooks for repeated tasks

## Architecture overview

The structure combines docs, runbooks, and guardrails so agent execution is constrained by explicit acceptance criteria.

## Current status

Active and evolving as new production lessons are captured.

## Why it matters

Harness engineering is often the bottleneck between prototype quality and dependable software delivery.
