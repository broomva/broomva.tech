You cannot have meaningful autonomy without meaningful governance.

The agents that work best in production are the ones operating inside repos with strong contracts, comprehensive tests, and explicit acceptance criteria. The governance infrastructure is what makes autonomy safe.

Invest in the harness. The autonomy follows.
